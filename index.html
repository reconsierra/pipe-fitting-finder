Thread ID – Visual + Vernier Proof of Concept

Pick what it looks like first, then confirm with vernier-friendly measurement ranges (mm). This is a workshop identification aid, not a machining spec.

1) Visual check

Select the path that matches what you can see. (Heuristic: metric is often parallel + O‑ring; imperial pipe threads are often tapered.)

2) What are you measuring?

Choose the fitting end type. You can leave any measurement as “Not sure / can’t measure”.

3) Select the closest measured range (all in mm)

Tip: For female openings, the inside jaws of a vernier tend to land on the thread roots, so the value is closer to the minor diameter than the major. That’s why the ranges here use minor / tap-drill proxies.
